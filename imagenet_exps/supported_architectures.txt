The current implementation supports different variants of ResNet-18 architecture.

LPF ResNet models: 
'resnet18_lpf1', 'resnet18_lpf2', 'resnet18_lpf3', 'resnet18_lpf4', 'resnet18_lpf5'.

APS ResNet models: 
'resnet18_aps1', 'resnet18_aps2', 'resnet18_aps3', 'resnet18_aps4', 'resnet18_aps5'.

'resnet18_lpf1' corresponds to baseline downsampling.
